Hoplite Tactics Online

Getting Started

    Hoplite Tactics Online is a two player real-time strategy game. Click 'Play Online' to view availabe players. Select a player to invite to a game session. The game session will begin once game invite has been accepted by other player. The global chat loby may also be used to find players and coordinate game sessions. Game session results can be viewed by clicking on the 'Rankings' tab.


    Controls

    • Movement - Arrow keys move selected unit up, down, left, or right.
    • Select Unit - 'z' key toggle selection between three hoplite units. Only selected unit is controlled.
    • Idle - 'z' key activate stance. All units begin game in Idle stance.
    • Phalanx - 'g' key activate stance.
    • Turtle - 't' key activate stance.
    • Strike - 'r' key (quick attack) from any stance.
    • Fight - 'f' key activate from Phalanx stance only.
    • Push - Arrow keys while Phalanx stance is active, and colliding with enemy hoplite unit.


      • Game Mechanics

      • Each player will start the game with three hoplite units to command
      • Each hoplite unit has individual Status Bars, Stances, Abilities, and Controls
      • Status Bars:
      • Health - Green represents remaining health. Red fills bar as unit receives damage. Unit dies when bar is 100% red.
      • Stamina (Endurance) - Blue represents remaining endurance stamina. Used for unit 'Movement', 'Fight', and 'Push' Abilities. Stamina recovers over time. Recovery rate depends on stance and current actions.
      • Stamina (Explosive) - Purple represents remaining explosive stamina. Used for 'Strike' attack. Stamina recovers over time. Recovery rate depends on stance and current actions.
      • Stances:
      • Idle - Each unit will begin the game in this stance. Increased movement speed to change position quickly. Increased stamina recovery speed. Most vulnerable to all attacks.
      • Phalanx - Toggle into defensive stance against hoplite units. Reduced movement speed. Least vulnerable to hoplite attacks. Vulnerable to archer attacks. Requires Stamina (Endurance) ≥ 1% to remain active. If Stamina is below 1%, unit will return to Idle stance.
      • Turtle - Toggle into defensive stance against archer units. Reduced movement speed. Vulnerable to hoplite attacks. Least vulnerable to archer attacks.
      • Abilities:
      • Strike - Quick attack. Deals most damage. Takes 50% Stamina (Endurance), and 100% Stamina (Explosive)
      • Fight - Toggle attack mode. Deals minimal damage, and takes minimal Stamina (Endurance) while active. Requires Stamina (Endurance) ≥ 10% to remain active.
      • Push - Forcefully move enemy hoplite unit. Activated while in Phalanx stance, moving forward, and colliding with enemy hoplite unit. Takes maximum Stamina (Endurance) while active.
      • Victory Conditions
      • Capture enemy flag
      • Whichever player occupies their capture point first will win the match.
      • Both players will have capture points on the opposite side of the map.
      • A hoplite unit occupies their capture point by remaining on the capture point position for 15 seconds.
      • Eliminate all enemy units
      • Whichever player reduces all enemy unit health bars to 0% will win the match.
      • Obstacles
      • Map edges: Player's units will not be able to move beyond the Top, bottom, left, and right borders of the map.
      • Rocks: Two rock formations in the middle of the map to create choke points and protection from Archer attacks.
      • Archers: Archers are positioned at the top of the map preventing freedom of movement for hoplite units looking to rush across the map to capture points. Strategy is required to outmaneuver archers while maintaining hoplite offense and defense against opposing player. Archer position may be captured by a single hoplite unit, providing freedom of movement for other units. Archer attack range is limited to the top half of the map, allowing for hoplite units to move through narrow paths created by rock formations at the bottom of the map.
